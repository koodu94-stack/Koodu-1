About Us

Independence Layout's slow-burn favourite

Rewind Luxury Place is a relaxed dine-in, takeaway, and delivery spot in Independence Layout, Enugu — the kind of place where dinner quietly turns into drinks, and drinks turn into a game night you didn't plan for.

Good food, good company, and a room that knows how to hold both. Expect to spend around ₦10,000–₦20,000 per person, and no pressure to rush off — we're just getting going when other places start closing up.
